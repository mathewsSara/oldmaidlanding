How to Play Old Maid

How many players?

2 or More. You may play with others online or add bot players.

What do I need to play?

A standard 52 card deck, with 3 of the Queens removed. There should be 49 cards total.

What are the controls?

Click on the cards.

Rules

Win Condition: The player(s) without any cards left will win.

Three Queens are removed before the deck of cards is divided among the players. The cards do not need to be evenly distributed. 49 cards will be in play.

Every player will remove matching pairs from their hand and discard them. This will be done automatically for you in this game.

Once all players have finished finding the pairs in their hand, the dealer will allow the player to their left to pick a random card from their hand. Once the player has selected a card, the player will see if it has a match. Matching cards will be discarded automatically for you. The player will then allow the player to their left to pick a card. This cycle repeats until the unpaired Queen is the last card standing!
